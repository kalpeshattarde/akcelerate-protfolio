# Echofy

Download

Echofy is a modern music streaming app designed to explore, play, and manage music with ease. A clean interface brings discovery, search, and playback together through a smooth, responsive experience. Users can browse trending tracks, build playlists, adjust sound settings, and enjoy seamless playback through a focused music player. Everything is organized to showcase music clearly, making listening feel immersive, intuitive, and effortless.
