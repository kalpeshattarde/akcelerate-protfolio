# Intake Cal

Download

Intake is a focused calorie tracking app designed to make daily nutrition simple and measurable. Users can set targets, log meals quickly, and see calories remaining at a glance through a clean, distraction free dashboard. Macro and water tracking, streaks, and progress history stay clearly organized, helping users build consistent habits and stay aware of their intake over time. Everything is designed to reduce friction and keep nutrition tracking easy to maintain day after day.
