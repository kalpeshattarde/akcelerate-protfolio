# VitalTracker

Download

VitalTracker is a calm, modern health tracking app designed to keep daily wellness simple and clear. With soft visuals, balanced layouts, and an intuitive flow, it helps users track activity, nutrition, hydration, and sleep without friction. Health data stays easy to read, thoughtfully organized, and presented in a way that feels supportive rather than overwhelming. Insights update in real time, helping users build awareness and maintain healthy routines over time.
