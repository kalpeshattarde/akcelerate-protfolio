# Quotalytics

Download

Quotalytics is a sales performance analytics platform built to give teams a clear, actionable view of revenue activity. A centralized dashboard brings pipelines, targets, forecasts, and historical performance together in one place. Metrics update in real time, making it easy to spot trends, track progress against goals, and identify gaps early. Everything is designed to support confident decision making, accurate reporting, and consistent sales execution across teams.
