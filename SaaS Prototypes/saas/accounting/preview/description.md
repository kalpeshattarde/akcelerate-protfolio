# Ledgerly

Download

Ledgerly is an all in one accounting platform for small businesses and independent professionals. Handle bookkeeping, invoicing, bank reconciliation, and tax workflows from a single, streamlined interface. Import data from Excel or QuickBooks, automate recurring invoices and reminders, and track cash flow, profit and loss, and trial balance in real time. Role based access and audit logs ensure security, accuracy, and compliance as operations grow.
