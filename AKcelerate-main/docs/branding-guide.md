## Logo

The AKcelerate logo consists of:
- A pulse/activity icon (SVG) in white on a blue-to-cyan gradient background
- Wordmark: "AK" in dark navy + "celerate" in blue-to-cyan gradient
- Font: Poppins Bold

### Logo Files
Place logo files in `/brand-kit/logo/`:
- `logo-full-light.svg` — Full logo for light backgrounds
- `logo-full-dark.svg` — Full logo for dark backgrounds
- `logo-icon.svg` — Icon only
- `logo-white.svg` — All-white version for dark overlays

## Voice & Tone

- **Professional** — Enterprise B2B tone
- **Confident** — "We deliver results" not "We try to"
- **Data-driven** — Always back claims with numbers
- **Clear** — No jargon overload; explain technical concepts simply
